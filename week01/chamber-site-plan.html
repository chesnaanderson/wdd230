Bountiful Chamber of Commerce

Site Purpose

The Bountiful Chamber of Commerce will be a place for people and businesses alike to come and disocver more about Bountiful Utah. People will be able to find business-to-business networking, events, and other details they might need.

Scenarios:

  • What events will the chamber be holding this month that promote business-to-business networking?
  • Where can I find contact information for the chamber's board of directors?
  • What has been the population growth in the area?
